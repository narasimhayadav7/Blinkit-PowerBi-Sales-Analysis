## Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing Blinkit's grocery sales data to understand
sales performance, customer satisfaction, and inventory distribution.
The objective is to identify key insights and opportunities for business
optimization using Power BI.

## Business Requirement
The main requirement of this project is to conduct a comprehensive analysis of:
- Sales performance
- Customer ratings
- Outlet and inventory distribution

The dashboard is designed to help business users monitor performance and
make data-driven decisions.

## Key KPIs
- **Total Sales**: Overall revenue generated from all items sold  
- **Average Sales**: Average revenue per sale  
- **Number of Items**: Total number of items sold  
- **Average Rating**: Average customer rating of items  

## Analytical Requirements
The dashboard addresses the following analysis areas:

1. **Total Sales by Fat Content**  
   - Analyze the impact of fat content on total sales  
   - Compare KPIs across low-fat and regular items  

2. **Total Sales by Item Type**  
   - Identify top-performing product categories  
   - Evaluate sales contribution by item type  

3. **Fat Content by Outlet for Total Sales**  
   - Compare sales across outlets based on fat content  

4. **Total Sales by Outlet Establishment**  
   - Analyze how outlet establishment year impacts sales  

5. **Percentage of Sales by Outlet Size**  
   - Understand the relationship between outlet size and sales contribution  

6. **Sales by Outlet Location**  
   - Assess sales performance across different location tiers  

7. **All Metrics by Outlet Type**  
   - Compare total sales, average sales, number of items, and ratings
     across different outlet types  

## Tools Used
- Power BI
- Excel
- DAX

## Dashboard
The dashboard includes interactive visuals such as KPI cards, bar charts,
donut charts, line charts, and tables. Filters are provided to enable
dynamic analysis based on outlet size, location, and item type.

## Conclusion
This Power BI dashboard provides a complete overview of Blinkit's sales
performance and supports data-driven decision-making by highlighting
key trends, customer preferences, and outlet performance.
